Wisdom Meter Explanation

The Wisdom Meter is a tool designed to help you assess your daily actions and decisions through a series of reflective questions. Each question focuses on key aspects of wisdom, encouraging self-discipline, empathy, and thoughtful decision-making.

By rating your responses, you can gain insights into your wisdom level for the day. A higher score reflects more wise choices and actions. This tool aims to promote personal growth and mindfulness in your daily life.

Scores range from 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest).

Use the Wisdom Meter to track your progress over time and strive to improve your wisdom in various situations!
